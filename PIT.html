About USTP

The University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines (USTP) is a state university established on August 16, 2016, by virtue of Republic Act 10919 through the amalgamation of the Mindanao University of Science and Technology (MUST) in Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental and the Misamis Oriental State College of Agriculture and Technology (MOSCAT) in Claveria, Misamis Oriental. It is located in Northern Mindanao, the Gateway to Mindanao, which offers a strategic locational advantage for the institution to train and develop students from all the other regions.

Adhering to its general mandate to primarily provide advanced education; higher technological, professional, and advanced instruction; and advanced research and extension work, required for global competitiveness, the University envisions becoming a nationally recognized S&T University providing the vital link between education and the economy. As the university fulfills this mandate, it moves from within its immediate context and toward its larger international environment, as the first and only national university of science and technology in the country. It will operate as such that it will have seamless knowledge and collaboration with its stakeholders in the private and public sectors, the labor market, business, and industry.

USTP SYSTEM CAMPUSES

Main Campus

USTP ALUBIJID

USTP Alubijid is considered the Main Campus of the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines. It is strategically located at the center of Western Misamis Oriental in Barangay Lourdes, Alubijid.

It has a total land area of 292 hectares and is where the AGILA Science and Technology Park will soon rise. USTP Alubijid offers the following programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering, Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering, and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

Visit USTP Alubijid

Major Campuses

USTP CAGAYAN DE ORO

USTP Cagayan de Oro is one of the university systems major campuses strategically located at the center of the City of Golden Friendship with a total land area of 9 hectares.

The campus is CHED-recognized as a Center of Excellence in Information Technology and Center of Development in Electrical Engineering and Mathematics. It is also acknowledged as a Top 4 Performing SUC with the Highest Number of Level III Accredited Programs in 2020 as adjudged by the AACCUP, Inc. Its niche programs include Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, and Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics.

Visit USTP Cagayan de Oro
View 360 Virtual Tour

USTP CLAVERIA

USTP Claveria is another major campus under the USTP System strategically located at the heart of Eastern Misamis Oriental in Poblacion, Claveria. It has a total land area of 104 hectares and is the future location of the AGROPOLIS Science and Technology Park.

The campus is CHED-recognized as a Center of Development in Agriculture and is acknowledged as a Top 4 Performing SUC with the Highest Number of Level III Accredited Programs in 2020 as adjudged by AACCUP, Inc.

USTP Claverias niche programs include the Bachelor of Science in Agriculture and the Bachelor of Science in Environmental Engineering.

Visit USTP Claveria
View 360 Virtual Tour

Satellite Campuses

USTP BALUBAL

USTP Balubal was established as one of the satellite campuses under the USTP System by virtue of Republic Act No. 10919 and supported by House Bill 424. It is located in Balubal, Cagayan de Oro City, and has a total area of one hectare.

The campus offers the following programs: Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Technology and Livelihood Education, and Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Technology.

Visit USTP Balubal

USTP JASAAN

USTP Jasaan is a satellite campus in Jasaan, Misamis Oriental. It has a total land area of 1.6 hectares and is the Universitys hub for Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering. It is the first of its kind in Mindanao and is touted to strengthen the countrys Marine Engineering and Naval Architecture landscape.

USTP Jasaan offers the following programs: Bachelor of Science in Energy Systems Management, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Manufacturing Engineering Technology, Bachelor of Science in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering, and Bachelor of Science in Technology Communication Management.

Visit USTP Jasaan
View 360 Virtual Tour

USTP OROQUIETA

USTP Oroquieta is a satellite campus located at Mobod, Oroquieta City in Misamis Occidental. It has a total land area of nine hectares.

The campus offers the following programs: Bachelor of Science in Food Processing and Technology, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, and Bachelor of Technology Livelihood Education.

Visit USTP Oroquieta
View 360 Virtual Tour

USTP PANAON

USTP Panaon is another satellite campus located in Punta, Panaon in Misamis Occidental. The campus has a total land area of 4.2 hectares.

USTP Panaon offers the following programs: Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Marine Biology, and Bachelor of Technology Livelihood Education Major in Home Economics and Major in Industrial Arts.

Visit USTP Panaon
View 360 Virtual Tour

USTP VILLANUEVA

USTP Villanueva is a satellite campus established in 2019 in accordance with Republic Act No. 11307. It is located in Poblacion 1, Villanueva in Misamis Oriental, and has a total land area of five hectares.

The campus offers the following programs: Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Technology Communication Management, and Bachelor of Science in Electro-Mechanical Technology.

Visit USTP Villanueva
